Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Please see below for a list of frequently asked questions. If you need further assistance, please contact technical support under the "Help" tab within the Scribr menu.

New, Open, Save, or Quit File

To begin a new file, open a working text file, save a current working file, or quit a file, simply locate the "File" tab in the upper left menu, and select the desired action. Please note, only .txt files can be opened in the Scribr application

  • Words
  • Sentences
  • Paragraphs
  • Sections
  • Chapters

What triggers the "Edits" to populate the menu on the right hand side of the application?

What triggers the edits depends on what type of edit it is. Words, and sentences, will be triggered by spaces, and periods, repsectively. So everytime you enter a word and subsequently hit space, that word will be added into the "Words" section of the edit menu. Likewise for sentences, when you end a sentence with a period, that will trigger that sentence to be pushed onto the "Sentences" section of the edit menu.

Can I delete/undo multiple edits, from different edit types, at one time?

Absolutely! Simply select those edits across the different sections you'd like to delete, and voila, they're deleted, or undone.

I had a bit too much wine, and got a little crazy with chapter 5.. I want to undo all the chnages I made to a particular chapter. Can I do this in one go?

Yes. All you need to do is go to the "Undo" tab in the top menu bar, and select the type of edits you would like to do an "Undo All" of. You can also do this for deleting a type of edits. Just navigate to the "Delete" tab in the menu bar and follow the same procedure.
